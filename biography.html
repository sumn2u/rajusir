Raju Gurung is a Nepali filmmaker and musician living and working in Kathmandu. He produces and directs both feature films and documentaries. As a musician and composer, he writes jazz/global fusion music, documentary film scores, and contemporary Nepali music. His principal instruments are drums and other percussion. He is an accomplished guitarist, and has led several award-winning rock and jazz/fusion bands in Nepal.

Musical background

Raju began playing music professionally at the age of 15. He was awarded a full scholarship and graduated from Berklee College of Music in the U.S. He has released two CDs of original music: an album of original fusion music with his band Equinox, and a solo album.
